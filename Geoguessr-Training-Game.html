Landscape instant clues

Treeless volcanic moss = Iceland/Faroe. Atacama desert = northern Chile. Eucalyptus/red dirt = Australia. Green mountains/sheep = New Zealand.

Use meta carefully

Google car, camera generation, roof racks, and blur are useful, but can change. Use them as confirmation, not your only clue.
